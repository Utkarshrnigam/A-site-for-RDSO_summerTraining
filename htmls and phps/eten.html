GENERAL INFORMATION ABOUT E-TENDERING


Take Digital Signature Certificate and Connect with e-tendering system

  

  1. All the supply, works as well as services e-tenders of RDSO are being floated through e-procurement web portal www.ireps.gov.in
  2. In order to participate e-tenders, vendors are required to get themselves registered with IREPS (www.ireps.gov.in)
  3. Registration with IREPS will enable you to participate in e-tenders of Indian Railways.
  4. For registration with IREPS, please obtain class III Digital Signature from prescribed certifying agencies (CA) and refer Railway’s website www.ireps.gov.in for further details for getting your firms registered with IREPS website.
