RDSO Central Library is located on the 1st floor of Annexe I (Manak Bhavan) building. Library has important collection of books, bound periodicals, reports, specifications and other technical publications. At present there are about 28,000 books, 60,000 standards and about 8,000 bound volumes of journals. In addition, there are about 20,000 reports related to RDSO, Railway Board and UIC etc.
