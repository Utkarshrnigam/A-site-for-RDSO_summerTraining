Visit of Transformation Cell officials to RDSO

Shri Sudheer Kumar, Principal Executive Director/Transformation alongwith other Senior Officers from the Transformation Cell of Railway Board visited RDSO on 25/04/2018. The objective of the team was to deliberate on measures to be taken for transforming RDSO into a Centre of Excellence for Railway Research.

The team interacted with Shri M. Hussain, Director General/RDSO, Shri H. K. Singh, Additional Director General/RDSO and all other Senior Officers in a meeting of over 3 hours. The key points of discussion for transforming RDSO were on enhancing the powers of Director General, Segregation of Long-term & Short-term research, strengthening the existing Labs and establishing new labs, Induction policy of officers to RDSO, review of Tenure system of posting in RDSO, acquiring of higher studies, attending technical training programmes/seminars within India and abroad, initial training programme for newly inducted officials etc. It was decided that concrete proposals on each of these shall be sent by RDSO to Railway Board, on which quick decision is expected.
